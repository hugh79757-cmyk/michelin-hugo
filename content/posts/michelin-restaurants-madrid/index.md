At the pinnacle of the Michelin ratings in Madrid is DiverXO, the only restaurant in the city to hold three stars. Under the visionary leadership of chef Dabiz Muñoz, DiverXO presents a creative menu that pushes boundaries, featuring dishes like “Galician lobster waking up on the beaches of Goa” and “drunken crabs partying in J.” This restaurant is a testament to culinary artistry and innovation.

The two-star category is home to six remarkable establishments, each offering a unique dining experience. Ramón Freixa Atelier stands out with its creative haute cuisine, where renowned chef Ramón Freixa crafts exquisite dishes in an exclusive setting. Coque, led by the Sandoval brothers, combines creativity with a warm dining atmosphere, while DSTAgE, situated in an industrial loft, offers a striking environment alongside its inventive menu. Paco Roncero embodies a relentless pursuit of culinary excellence, making his restaurant a worth trying for any serious food lover. Smoked Room presents a contemporary dining experience reminiscent of clandestine spaces, where every meal is a unique journey. Lastly, Deessa, the gourmet standard-bearer of the Mandarin Oriental Ritz, showcases a creative approach to fine dining.

## One-Star Gems

Madrid's one-star restaurants are a great destination of culinary experiences, each offering distinct flavors and styles. EMi, located in the heart of Chamberí, focuses on modern cuisine with a touch of elegance. For those who appreciate Japanese cuisine, Sen Omakase is a top choice, emphasizing the artistry of presentation. Èter introduces diners to a contemporary setting inspired by the concept of the sky, while La Tasquería elevates humble offal to new heights, thanks to chef Javi Estévez's innovative approach.

![Photo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/michelin-restaurants-madrid/body_3.jpg)
*Photo by [Travel Photographer](https://www.pexels.com/@travel-photographer-127255675) on [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com)*

Saddle, with its nod to equestrian culture, blends modern and classic cuisine, while Gofio showcases regional flavors in a contemporary light. Yugo The Bunker offers an authentic izakaya experience, and A'Barra presents an international menu centered around premium products. Quimbaya takes diners on a culinary journey through Colombian flavors, while Ricardo Sanz Wellington impresses with its Japanese contemporary offerings.

Clos Madrid, helmed by sommelier Marcos Granda, is a modern cuisine standout, and Santerra specializes in La Mancha cuisine, making it a great spot for those seeking regional specialties. Gaytán is the realization of chef Javier Aranda's dream, while Corral de la Morería Gastronómico combines modern cuisine with the allure of flamenco. OSA, although a bit off the beaten path, offers a charming dining experience by the river, while Toki serves up refined Japanese dishes. 

Chispa Bistró, with its industrial vibe, is perfect for a casual yet sophisticated meal, while El Invernadero positions itself as a leader in vegetarian and vegan cuisine under the guidance of chef Rodrigo de la Calle. VelascoAbellà highlights farm-to-table principles, and Ugo Chan provides a fusion experience with a contemporary touch. Finally, Pabú and CEBO round out the one-star offerings with their creative menus, while RavioXO, known for its dumplings, encapsulates the luxury of time in its culinary approach.

## Cuisine Styles You Will Find in Madrid

The culinary landscape of Madrid is diverse, featuring a variety of styles that reflect both tradition and innovation. Traditional cuisine remains strong, with 20 restaurants dedicated to classic Spanish dishes. Contemporary and creative cuisines also flourish, with 15 and 11 establishments respectively. Japanese cuisine is well-represented with 10 restaurants, while modern cuisine holds its ground with another 10 options. 

Fusion and farm-to-table concepts are gaining traction, with 9 and 5 restaurants respectively, showcasing the city's adaptability and willingness to embrace new trends. Meats and grills, international, and Japanese contemporary styles further enrich the dining scene, providing a range of flavors and experiences for every palate.

## Price Ranges and What to Expect

In Madrid, the price range for Michelin-starred dining varies, with most establishments falling into the €€€€ category. This pricing reflects the quality of ingredients, the creativity of the dishes, and the overall dining experience. Expect to pay a premium for the three-star DiverXO, while two-star and one-star restaurants offer a more varied range, often providing exceptional value for the quality of food and service.

Dining in these Michelin-starred restaurants typically involves a multi-course experience, allowing guests to savor a variety of flavors and presentations. Many establishments also offer tasting menus, which can be an excellent way to experience the chef's signature dishes.

## How to Book and Tips for Dining

Reservations are highly recommended for Michelin-starred restaurants in Madrid, as they tend to fill up quickly. It is advisable to book well in advance, especially for popular spots like DiverXO and Ramón Freixa Atelier. Many restaurants offer online booking options, but calling directly can sometimes yield better results or special arrangements.

When dining at these esteemed establishments, consider opting for the tasting menu, which often showcases the chef's best creations. Additionally, pairing your meal with wine can enhance the overall experience, so don't hesitate to ask for recommendations from the sommelier.

Dress codes may vary, but a smart casual approach is generally a safe bet. Lastly, be prepared for a leisurely dining experience, as meals at Michelin-starred restaurants are often designed to be savored rather than rushed.

 Madrid's Michelin dining scene offers a rich array of options for food enthusiasts, from the pinnacle of culinary artistry at DiverXO to the innovative and accessible fare found in one-star and Bib Gourmand establishments. Whether you are a local or a visitor, exploring these restaurants will undoubtedly provide a memorable insight into the city's lively food culture.
